SOUND ARCHITECT

Paolo Petrillo operates as a Sound Architect and Strategic Consultant working on Sound Governance, Identity Systems and Sonic Positioning. His work sits at the intersection of architecture, neuroscience and brand strategy.

Sound is not a layer. It is infrastructure.

Sound is not merely entertainment. It is an invisible, high-impact infrastructure that bypasses rational filters and directly engages neurophysiology, transforming user experience into measurable brand equity.

This shift moves sound from aesthetic intuition to strategic asset.

From Sonic Anarchy to Strategic Control

The current use of sound in branded environments is often fragmented, driven by subjective taste and isolated decisions. Sound Governance introduces structure: a system where sonic identity becomes measurable, consistent and scalable.

Diagnosis, Infrastructure, Governance — three layers that transform perception into controlled strategic value.

Strategic Practice

The consultancy operates independently from production studios to ensure neutrality in decision-making. This allows sound strategy to remain aligned with brand governance rather than execution bias.

Selected environments include institutional spaces, international events and brand ecosystems where sound defines memory and perception.
